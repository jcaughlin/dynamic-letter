Under Wis. Admin Code DCF 12. Caregiver Law and Wis. Stat. 48.686, Individuals found guilty of certain                        crimes and other offenses may require a Rehabilitation Review approval before that person may receive                         a license or be employed at or reside in a child care center. If you wish to apply for a Rehabilitation Review pursuant to DCF 12, the application can                         be found at                         https://dcf.wisconsin.gov/rehab-review
